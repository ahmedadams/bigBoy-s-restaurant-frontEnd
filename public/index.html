Big Boy's Burger

We are Big Boy Burgers est 1980

We have burger restaurants in London, Reading, Bristol, Cambridge, Cardiff, Brighton, Manchester and Liverpool. We make British beef patties in our own butchery and rosemary chips are cut and cooked daily.Simple and versatile, this recipe for rosemary salt goes all the way back to the beginning when Tom and Phil were planning their first burger restaurant.

Every restaurant serves local beers from neighbouring breweries, and many of our restaurants serve local burgers using ingredients from nearby food heroes. Together with Brighton friends Laine Brew Co we’ve brewed a British lager that stands up to our burgers: a refreshing beer with balanced hops, light bitterness and a biscuity malt sweetness that complements our food.
